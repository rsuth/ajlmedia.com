What We Do

  • Let us worry about the presentation so that you can focus on your case. With a "hot seat" trial tech, all you have to do is call out an exhibit and it will appear on the screen in under a second. Our trial techs are experts at "on the fly" video editing, so that any part of your videotaped depositions can be played at any time, making impeaching the witness much more impactful.

  • Don't let old, consumer grade equipment come between your evidence and the jurors evaluating your case. AJL carries only top of the line, Full HD equipment at competitive rates. We work with court staff to set up all presentation equipment before you arrive, so that all you have to do is plug in and go.

  • Keep your jurors engaged when the issues get complex. Help them understand your case the way you want them to. Our experienced graphic designers work with you to create perfect demonstratives. We have experience creating timelines, charts, and other visuals that stick.

  • Depositions, Settlement videos, Day in the Life videos, Site inspections. AJL productions start with great videographers and top of the line equipment. We bring 34 years of broadcast quality video recording and editing to your case.

  • Surveillance camera and cell phone footage is present in an increasing number of trials. AJL may be able to increase the clarity of this type of evidence by using state of the art techniques. We can apply effects such as image stabilization, lighting enhancements, crop/zoom, highlighting, and slow motion to aid the jury's interpretation.

  • Have a new and different idea for trial but not sure where to begin? Give us a call! We've worked with attorneys to create new and innovative ways of presenting and managing digital evidence. We are happy to provide support on any technical problem you might have. Video not playing? Powerpoint not showing up correctly? Odds are we've seen it before.

About Us

AJL Litigation Media was originally founded in 1981 by August Larsen to provide the San Diego legal community with high quality video taping and production services. As technology has changed the way cases are tried AJL has constantly evolved to bring the highest technical expertise to the handling and presentation of digital evidence. In 2016, AJL Litigation Media, Inc. became an employee owned and operated company, and transformed into AJL Media LLC.

Principals Rick Sutherland and Robert Jordan have played an integral role in hundreds of jury trials, with hundreds of millions of dollars at stake. They are proud to be trusted by many of the finest attorneys in Southern California.

About Us

AJL Media was originally founded in 1981 to provide the San Diego legal community with high quality video taping and production services. As technology has changed the way cases are tried AJL has constantly evolved to bring the highest technical expertise to the handling and presentation of digital evidence.

Principals Rick Sutherland and Robert Jordan have played an integral role in hundreds of jury trials, with hundreds of millions of dollars at stake. They are proud to be trusted by many of the finest attorneys in Southern California.
